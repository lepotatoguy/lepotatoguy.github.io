About Me

Hello, I am Joyanta!

I am from Dhaka, Bangladesh; recently completed my undergraduate studies having a major in Computer Science. My research interests include Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Cloud Computing, Modeling & Simulation and Human-Computer Interaction. My vision is to solve problems and design low-cost/handy approaches for social welfare and sustainability using the power of state-of-the-art technologies. Currently, as a researcher, I am pursuing research on different projects, allowing me to dive deep into the sections I am interested. I have co-authored several conference papers and presented my research papers in different well-reputed conferences. My researches have been published in premier journal of cloud computing (IEEE TCC) and conference (COMPASS). Also, I achieved several research awards covering participation grants, travel grants, etc. Outside of my academic interests, I love to play games, watch TV Shows and anime. I love immersing myself in the virtual worlds of my favorite video games and challenging my friends to epic battles. I am also a big fan of outdoor activities and try to go cycling whenever I get the chance.
