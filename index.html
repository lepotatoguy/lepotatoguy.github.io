About Me

Hello, I am Joyanta!

I am from Dhaka, Bangladesh; a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science under the guidance of Dr. Yuliang Zheng at The University of Alabama at Birmingham. Currently, my research work focuses on understanding and exploring the Trustworthiness of Artificial Intelligence, including the Privacy, Safety and Security, with a specific emphasis on Large Language Models like ChatGPT. Other research interests include Computer Vision, Cloud Computing, Modeling & Simulation and Human-Computer Interaction. At present, as a dedicated researcher, I am actively engaged in exploring different projects that allow me to delve deeper into the areas that captivate my interest. I have co-authored several conference papers and presented my research papers in different well-reputed conferences. My researches have been published in premier journal of cloud computing (IEEE TCC) and conference (COMPASS). In addition to doing research, I have the experience of actively participating as a reviewer in the peer-review process of research papers at different confernce (NeurIPS, ICLR) and journal (ACM JCSS). Also, I have been honored with several research awards covering participation grants, travel grants, etc. Outside of my academic interests, I love to play games, watch TV Shows and anime. I love immersing myself in the virtual worlds of my favorite video games and challenging my friends to epic battles. I am also a big fan of outdoor activities and try to go cycling whenever I get the chance.
